Dynamic CAPE Efficiency

Instructions: Enter exactly two of the three inputs below and optionally the number of lightning strikes per minute. The script will calculate the missing parameter and CAPE efficiency, capped at 90%.

Updraft speed is calculated as:

w = α × √(2 × CAPE) × D

where \( \alpha \) (efficiency) depends on lightning strikes per minute, capped at 90%, and \( D \) is the drag factor.
